Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of numbers, patterns, and relationships. It is an essential tool in various fields such as science, engineering, and finance. From basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, mathematics helps in solving real-world problems and making informed decisions.

Science

Science involves exploring the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis. It encompasses disciplines like physics, chemistry, and biology. Science has significantly contributed to technological advancements, improving our understanding of the universe.

History

History is the study of past events and civilizations. It helps us understand the social, political, and cultural evolution of humanity. Learning about history allows us to appreciate our heritage and draw lessons from past successes and failures.

English

English is an artistic expression of ideas, emotions, and culture through written works such as novels, poems, and plays. It inspires creativity, enriches our understanding of human experiences, and allows us to connect with different perspectives.
