Exploring Cities

Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, has a well-developed education system. The city is home to several top-ranked universities and institutions, including the University of Delhi, Jawaharlal Nehru University, and Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. The city also has a number of educational boards, such as the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS).

Mumbai

Mumbai, being the financial capital of India, places a strong emphasis on education. The city is home to several prestigious institutions, including the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT Bombay), the National Centre for Advanced Research (NCAR), and the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR).

Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, India, has a well-developed education system. The city is home to several prestigious educational institutions, including universities, colleges, and schools. Some of the top educational institutions in Jaipur include the University of Rajasthan, Manipal University, and Jaipur National University.

Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad, the largest city in the state of Gujarat, India, is known for its rich educational heritage. The city is home to several prestigious educational institutions, including the Gujarat University, which was established in 1949. Other notable institutions in Ahmedabad include the Indian Institute of Management (IIM), the National Institute of Design (NID), and the Gujarat Technological University (GTU).
